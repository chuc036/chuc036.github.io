Miramui Re-brand
Project Date: Spring 2021
Logo re-design, branding, typography

Miramui was a sustainable luxury fashion capsule collection originally created by Zorina Wong. In 2021, Zorina and Jehan Vazifdar collaborated to relaunch the brand with a new capsule collection. I was asked to update the logo to reflect the brandâ€™s new styles and later assisted with the setup of their shop and website.

Original logo by Erkhes Batjargal

The original logo broke the word into two parts for a vertical format, and focused on a more illustrative style and neutral color palette. Miramui's new goal is to connect with consumers with a contemporary sense of dress, while maintaining the ethical and sustainable goal of the brand. The new logo is a custom typeface that reflects the curvature of the garments and the accents plays with the height of each letterform.
