Indoor Kids Poster
Project Date: Winter 2023
typography, poster design, client work

Noa Rosenberg and JR Atkins are two comedians and good friends that have brought their show, Indoor kids to The Edinburgh Fringe Festival in 2022 and 2023.

I was asked to help with a quick poster and rollout for their show. I focused on constructing a type treatment that followed the show's focus on childhood and personal growth.
