Commandment Posters
A physical version of statements from one of my web projects, The Cinematic Commandment Generator. Narratives are often extremely simplified for easy consumption. These story beats, character tropes, visual language and motifs are elements of an ever-productive formula.
However, as expansive as the possibilities of narratives are, they also suffer from the repetition present and integral to the entertainment industry. By randomizing these traits, I tried to capture the absurdities of its language and juxtapose these tropes with clear and uninterrupted typography.
Killer Joe Poster Concepts
Two poster concepts for the play Killer Joe by Tracy Letts.
Cast and Location taken from the Trafalgar Studios 2018 production.
Killer Joe is a thriller play about a family seeking to take a hit out on their own mother for insurance money. To do so, they hire a diabolical hitman that they aren't prepared for. The play is tense, dark, and sometimes funny, and focuses on themes of american violence, media sensationalism, and masculinity.
Vazifdar Branding and Logo
In my first foray into design for fashion, I collaborated with Jehan Vazifdar to make their logo and graphics for their fashion thesis presentation and portfolio website on Squarespace.



The collection interprets traditional Parsi garments through queered tailoring techniques. Jehan wanted a logo that bridged classic typographic forms like serifs, and the contemporary visuals of contemporary luxury fashion.
Using photos of the collection by Aiden Doyle and various process photos, Jehan and I made series of print Posters including the stages of the project and prototypes.
Experimenting with alternate methods of showing clothing during the COVID-19 pandemic, Jehan wrote a small entry for a catalog showing the garments and editorial photography.
For more on the project, please visit Jehan's website.

Omniuser Div Jewelry Collection
Miramui Relaunch Logo
Miramui is a fashion brand focused on sustainable and ethical direct-to-consumer handmade garments, started by my friend Zorina Wong. A year after opening, Zorina and Jehan Vazifdar decided to collaborate and are relaunching the brand with a new visual direction and target audience.


The new logo is a custom typeface that reflects the curviture of the garments and the accents above the i. The long letterforms are long and heavy, like draped laundry.



The original logo broke the word into two parts, and focused on a more illustrative style and neutral color palatte. Miramui's new goal is to connect with consumers with a contemporary sense of dress, while maintaining the ethical and sustainable goal of the brand.
Original Logo by Erkhes Batjargal
Omniuser Div Jewelry Line
Omniuser Div is a jewelry project about internet users and the superficial structure of front end HTML structure. The peices are made of crystal cupchain to mimic the rigid structure of web design, and are saudered in the shape of glyphs and UI elements. Some pieces were not able to go into production due to COVID-19, but I was able to complete the centerpieces of the collection.
This project challenged me to learn new skills while allowing me to approach the design with a concept that filled a specific purpose in the accessories market: Bold, Cheeky, modern statement jewelry focused towards denizens of silicon valley.
