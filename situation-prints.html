Situation Prints
Project Date: Fall 2021
typography, art direction, web interaction

These prints and the web generator were made for a part of my thesis project, an exploration of narrative construction through typography and language. Each statement is composed of elements of various narrative tropes. The website randomizes the statements and color format, and contains a comprehensive Netflix watch history from an exceptionally long uninterrupted watch session during the early weeks of COVID-19 lockdown.
