GPN Logo Design
Project Date: Fall 2022
Logo design, branding, typography

Global Polymer Network LLC is a distributor of secondary market plastics, including Engineering and commodity-grade materials like Cellulosics, PVC flex, EVA, TPE, Excess Virgin and Obsolete plastics.



With no prior logo or branding package, I worked with the owner to develop his concept, taking it from a simple sketch to a deliverable package containing all prior and current brand identities.



I was drawing inspiration from the super graphic industrial look, as well as directional letterforms, reminiscent of the directionality of the triple arrow recycling symbol. For more on GPN, visit their website here: Global Polymer Network, LLC.
