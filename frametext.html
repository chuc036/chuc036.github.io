My name is Christopher Chu and I'm a graphic designer in Brooklyn, New York.


I graduated with a BFA in communications design from Parsons School of Design in 2021. I specialize in editorial graphic design, including branding, typography, web design, illustration and printmaking. I also take various freelance work including portfolios, video projects, and posters.

My past clients include Hazon, Second Shift and Miramui.
