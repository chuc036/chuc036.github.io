Omniuser Div Branding
Project Date: spring 2020
jewelry design, branding


Omniuser Div is a jewelry brand I designed for an accessories design course. The collection was an assignment to create a brand from your accessories, which included a mission statement, marketing materials, and logos.

The collection was an assignment to create a brand from your accessories, which included a mission statement, marketing materials, and logos. My target audience was Silicone Valley, and I designed the jewelry around tech-company dress gestures like puffer jackets and corporate-approved clothing to create modern brooch and pin designs. My earlier designs featured sloped silver bars inspired by the blue Apple scrollbar.

This project also gave me the opportunity to learn about fashion accessory production pipelines. I learned how to create flats and specs, give production notes, and jewelry development. Although the assignment was mainly focused on the creation and form of the jewelry, I enjoyed the logo and advertising mockups I made.
